Profile

Who is Virat Kohli

A snapshot of the man behind the numbers — background, role, and what he's built across nearly two decades in international cricket.

Headline achievements

  • Most centuries in ODI cricket history (54), and second-most international centuries of all time (85), behind only Sachin Tendulkar.
  • Captained India to its first-ever Test series win in Australia (2018–19).
  • All-time leading run-scorer in IPL history, playing every season for Royal Challengers Bengaluru since 2008.
  • Part of three ICC title-winning squads: 2011 ODI World Cup, 2013 Champions Trophy, 2024 T20 World Cup.
  • Led RCB to back-to-back IPL titles in 2025 and 2026, ending the franchise's 18-year wait for a maiden trophy.
